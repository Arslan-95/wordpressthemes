Philosophy

Nutraceutical Excellence.

AURACOS brings 50 years of Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Expertize, cutting-edge products and unparalleled service to the field of Anti-aging & Cosmetic Nutraceuticals.

Company founder with their strong pharmaceutical background have sought to develop new high-level top quality nutraceuticals having an anti-aging proven effect on the Skin and the company is proud to introduce its fist product Pro.Collagenium on the global market in 2014. Today, the whole company is focused on developing new innovative products and is excited to let you discover its future pipeline of state-of-the-art Nutraceuticals.

Values

Strong core values set AURACOS apart. In everything we do, AURACOS is:
Quality-Oriented:

All of our products & ingredients have been extensively studied and meet the highest safety and Quality standards.

Exclusive-Minded:

We design Amazing products for Amazing people. Our products contain the best quality ingredients in the world having the most scientifically proven positive effect on the body.

R&D and Manufacturing-Involved:

We develop our own formulation thanks to our 20+ Scientists team and we do manufacture our products ourselves in our own GMP Pharmaceutical Facilities.

Prescriber-Focused:

To physicians & pharmacists, you’ll be proud to prescribe us. We create amazing new products, state-of-the-art packaging and educational support for the consumers. Our products showcase your advice and not the opposite.

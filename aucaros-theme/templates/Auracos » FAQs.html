Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who should take PRO.COLLAGENIUM?

PRO.COLLAGENIUM has been designed for adults who wish to promote healthy, good looking skin, hair and nails and who wish to counteract the visible signs of ageing.

2. Where can I buy the product?

PRO.COLLAGENIUM can be purchased in pharmacies in the following countries: Belgium, Switzerland, and the United Arab Emirates.

3. Who makes PRO.COLLAGENIUM?

Our product is manufactured in Morocco by the largest Pharmaceutical Manufacturer of the country, certified with an ISO 9001 Quality Management System and Good Manufacturing Practices (Pharmaceutical GMPs).

4. What is Collagen?

Collagen is the most abundant protein in the human body needed for the structure and density of the skin. Approximately 80% of young and healthy skin is made up of collagen, however, with time and natural ageing the body’s ability to replenish collagen decreases by about 1.5% per year.

5. What is the difference between the collagen in the skin and the collagen in the product?

The collagen naturally present in our skin, joints, tendons and ligaments has a high molecular weight (~300kDa). If applied on skin topically, it cannot be absorbed and if ingested, it is not easily digested or absorbed. The collagen used in our product is hydrolysed collagen (a smaller peptide form of collagen), which has a lower molecular weight (0.3 – 8KDa).  It is easily digested in the stomach and absorbed in the small intestine where it enters the blood stream and reaches the deeper layer of the skin.

6. How does PRO.COLLAGENIUM differ from an anti-ageing cream?

PRO.COLLAGENIUM stimulates the cells responsible for the body’s own natural production of collagen, elastin and moisture-retaining hyaluronic acid.  It is able to work in the deeper layer of the skin (dermis) and all over the body, whereas creams act on the outer layer of the skin (epidermis) only.

7. What is the right dosage when taking collagen?

Most of the collagen products available on the market contain 1 g, 3 g or 5 g of collagen per dosage. These dosages have no clinically proven positive effect on your skin as the only dosage that has shown clinically proven effect on the skin is 10 g per day.

8. Where does the collagen come from?

Auracos uses hydrolysed collagen extracted from fish skin; no other animal sources of collagen are used. All fish used to extract the collagen come from fish farms registered by the European Union for the import of edible fish and are deemed fit for human consumption and guaranteed by health certificates signed by official authorities. 

9. Why is the product in a liquid form rather than tablets?

Aside from being easier to swallow, a liquid is more readily absorbed as soon as it enters the stomach. Daily dosage (25ml) of PRO.COLLAGENIUM contains up to 10,000 mg of hydrolysed collagen which can be up to 40 times more than a typical collagen tablet. Tablets and capsules may cost less, but in reality, liquid represents far better value as more hydrolysed collagen can be absorbed and distributed to the body.

10. Does the product contain fish oils, porcine, chicken, beef, anti-biotics, hormones (or phytohormones or phytoestrogens), genetically modified organisms, use of nano-technology, gluten, alcohol, artificial preservatives, or artificial colours?

No, PRO.COLLAGENIUM doesn’t contain any of the above mentioned.

11. Does the product exert narcotic and psychotropic effects or have potent or drastic components, radioactive substances, banned food additives, antibiotics, insecticides and rodenticides?

No, PRO.COLLAGENIUM does not have any of the above mentioned.

12. Is it suitable for vegetarians?

PRO.COLLAGENIUM contains collagen proteins extracted from the skin of the fish.

13. Is the product halal?

Yes. We have a certificate from the Islamic and Cultural Centre certifying that the fish-extracted hydrolysed collagen used in PRO.COLLAGENIUM is halal. 

14. What does the product taste like?

As the product should be taken before your meal in the morning, PRO.COLLAGENIUM has a very unique, light caramel and lemon flavor that will perfectly match with your breakfast. You will love it!

15. Doesn’t the stomach acid destroy the collagen preventing it from reaching the dermis (skin’s deeper layer)?

No. The collagen peptides in our product are specifically formulated to retain their structural integrity and cannot be broken down by the stomach acid or enzymes. 

16. What is the recommended daily dose and best time of day to take the product?

For quicker absorption, drink 1 bottle of PRO.COLLAGENIUM on an empty stomach in the morning at breakfast. If you have a sensitive stomach, take it after food. You can mix it with water, milk or juice if necessary.

17. Can PRO.COLLAGENIUM be taken in combination with other food supplements?

Yes, however, please bear in mind that each product contains ingredients such as vitamin C that is already at the maximum recommended daily allowance (RDA, now replaced by NRV Nutrient Reference Value) so you need to ensure that you do not exceed your daily nutritional needs. It would be best to consult with your doctor or pharmacist to ensure you are receiving the optimal amount for your daily nutritional needs.

18. For how long do you need to use the product to see visible results?

The product results will vary depending on the individual and their starting skin, hair, health conditions, diet and lifestyle. According to our clinical trial results, with daily and consecutive use of the product (1 x 25 ml bottle), one can expect the skin to become more hydrated within 3 weeks, followed by a reduction in fine lines and deeper wrinkles within 6 weeks and an increase in skin elasticity within 9 weeks. Some individuals have reported to experience more positive results and/or at a faster rate than others.  Our recommendation is that the product should be taken for 3 months in order to realize all its benefits. Thereafter, it is a personal decision to continue, take a break or stop the treatment depending upon one’s own lifestyle.

19. What happens when I stop taking the product?

If you stop taking the product, the level of collagen slowly reverts back to its original level when the PRO.COLLAGENIUM treatment started.

20. Can I use the product if I am pregnant or breastfeeding?

There are no studies at present that support the use of food supplements during pregnancy or lactation. Therefore, it is not recommended, unless otherwise suggested by your doctor.

21. Can the product be taken with medication?

It is unlikely that they will adversely interact with any medication. However, we recommend that anyone suffering from a serious illness or those who are under medical supervision in general, consult their doctor prior to taking PRO.COLLAGENIUM.

22. Are there any side effects associated with PRO.COLLAGENIUM?

If you have experienced allergic reactions to fish or soya products, do not take PRO.COLLAGENIUM. Consuming too much collagen i.e. more than 15,000 mg (15 grams) per day may lead to a mild gastrointestinal upset.

23. Is the product suitable for diabetics?

No. In order to minimize the use of sweeteners, we use a mix of natural sugar (Fructose) and sweetener (Acesulfam K). Therefore, you should be consult your doctor prior to taking PRO.COLLAGENIUM and see if the quantity of sugar in the product can fit with your diet.

24. Is the product tested for quality and safety?

Yes. Laboratory tests are carried out on the finished product to ensure that it is safe for human consumption and meet pharmaceutical quality standards.
